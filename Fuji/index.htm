Overview of Camera Lens Types

XF18mmF2 R

The rearmost lens element, which is closest to the image sensor, effectively reduces the angle at which light enters the sensor.

XF16mmF2.8 R WR

The FUJINON XF16mmF2.8 R WR features a focal length equivalent to 24mm in the 35mm format and a maximum aperture of F2.8.

XF35mmF2 R WR

The FUJINON XF35mmF2 R WR lens offers a focal length equivalent to 53mm*, providing a natural field of view similar to that of the human eye.

XF35mmF1.4 R

The 5th molded aspherical glass element effectively reduces spherical aberration. In addition to delivering sharp focus across the image.
